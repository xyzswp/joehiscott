Joe Hiscott

Joe Hiscott has been teaching CFQ Qigong self-healing daily practice since 2010. He is a Certified CFQ Qigong Level 1 and Level 2 Instructor and offers ongoing training, weekly practice classes, bi-weekly zoom check-ins for both L1 and L2 students, monthly retreats and private healing sessions.  He is based in Rupert, Québec and also teaches in Kitchener, Ontario. Joe receives ongoing training with CFQ Founder Master Yap Soon Yeong in Penang, Malaysia.
